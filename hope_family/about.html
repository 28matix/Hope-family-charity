About The Hope Family Charity

The Hope Family Charity was founded in 2024 with a heartfelt mission: to bring hope, care, and support to vulnerable groups within the community—especially orphans and children in need.

The initiative began as a small community effort, led by compassionate youth determined to make a difference despite having limited resources. What started with simple acts of kindness—such as providing meals, clothing, and school supplies—grew into a more organized movement.

Driven by love, empathy, and a commitment to social change, the founders began organizing outreach events, fundraising, and awareness campaigns to expand their impact.

In just a short time, The Hope Family Charity became a symbol of unity and compassion, drawing in volunteers, well-wishers, and partners who shared the same vision.

Today, the charity continues to grow steadily, empowering the community to care for one another and nurturing a generation of youth who believe in giving hope where it's needed most.

Founder of The Hope Family Charity

Emmanuel Julius Juma, a passionate social worker and community leader, founded The Hope Family Charity in 2024. With a background in nonprofit management and a deep commitment to social justice, his leadership and dedication have been instrumental in shaping the charity's mission and expanding its reach within the community.
